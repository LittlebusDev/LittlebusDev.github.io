What is EQ

Emotional intelligence is a way of connecting with yourself, to become more aware of your emotions and reactions to certain stimuli. It’s a learned skill that we can harness and develop.

How is EQ different to IQ

EQ is more mastery of emotions, and IQ is a measure of your innate problem solving and analytical thinking abilities.

Why is EQ important?

EQ is important because all things can be conquerable when you have a certain level of self analysis. You can back yourself in your decisions, know where your emotional limits in a situation are and defend yourself, engage with others in a more meaningful way etc. These are all skills that will help develop you into a more well rounded person that can ultimately get more out of life than those that don’t try to develop their EQ.
